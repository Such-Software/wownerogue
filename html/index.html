    ❓ How to Play

    🎮 Game Objective

    Navigate through the dungeon, collect the treasure bag, and escape through the exit before the monster catches you or time runs out!

    ⌨️ Controls

    • WASD or Arrow Keys - Move your character
    • Enter - Join the queue to play

    🕐 Block Timing

    Games are synchronized to blockchain blocks. You may need to wait a few minutes for the next block before your game starts. The block height is shown at the top of the screen.

    💰 Payment Modes

    🎁 Payouts

    Set your payout address using the "Manage Payout Address" button. When you escape the dungeon, your winnings will be sent automatically. Escaping with the treasure bag earns a higher multiplier!
